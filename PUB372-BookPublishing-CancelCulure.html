[Book Proposal] Cancelled: The Fall of Celebrities

PDF Book Proposal

For this group project, we take the role of a publishing consulting company working together with an author to create a book proposal. The topics for this semster were: Covid19, Cancel Culture, and Indepent Publishers in Canada.

My team and I had choosen Cancel Culture for the topic. With the growing online community due to quarantine, we notice the increase of 'cancel culture' amongst celebrities & digital creators. In our book proposal, we created an ideal Canadian Influencer Persona that will act as our author of the proposed book.

Book Concept

Cancelled: The Fall of Celebrities by Celina Starke is a collection of essays that discuss the recent phenomena of cancel culture. Cancel culture can hit anyone at any time and recently, celebrities have not been able to keep their mouths shut. Beloved authors, actors, and comedians have shown their fans who they truly are... and it’s not pretty. J.K Rowling has expressed her transphobic values, Ellen DeGeneres does not actually preach her “be kind to one another” catchphrase, and Shane Dawson has finally cracked. All of these celebrities have been cancelled and Cancelled: The Fall of Celebrities gives you all the facts and a little bit of insight into what made these celebrities fall. Starke will discuss the lives of celebrities and how the pressure of their high status and gruelling life might have caused their inevitable downfall. Starke provides a deep dive into Hollywood while using her unique influencer perspective to weigh in on the lifestyle. Celina Starke’s non-fiction debut is a page-turning title that tackles the strange and fascinating lives of celebrities and how they have been cancelled.
