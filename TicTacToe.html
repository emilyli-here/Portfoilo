Notion Documentation

For the first group project in Material Design class, my team and I decided to make an interactive light piece. Additionally, our product needs to reflect the design culture of our choice of an existing company.

We had chosen Studio Brovhn playfulness with funcationalty from home designs Umbra. Our final product is an interactive TicTacToe light. To simplify the design of the classic game, we have used the squares and circles as playing piece. The light turns on when it comes close to the magnetic base.
