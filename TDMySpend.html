TD MySpend App Re-Design

View PDF Version

For my IAT 334 Final Individual project, we are to take an existing app and new features or re-design existing features. I had chosen the TD MySpend app as I have been a direct consumer of the application for years.

TD MySpend is a smart, easy-to-use app for iPhone and Android Smartphones that helps you monitor your spending from your TD Canadian dollar personal banking accounts and credit card accounts. As a unique money management tool, TD MySpend can help you make informed spending choices and find ways to save.
